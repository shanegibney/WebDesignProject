Brían Sparks is an Irish visual artist currently based in Dublin. Brían’s photography practice expresses his internal reflections on the themes of uncertainty, parentage and nature versus nurture. Based in various European cities for most of his adult life, Brían’s current projects focus on reconnecting with his past, adapting to contemporary Dublin life and his hopes for the future. Embracing ambiguity, welcoming the unexpected and valuing imperfection, Brían continues his photography practice as a method of self exploration.
